DATASETS USED IN SAT/GNN PAPERS - SUMMARY
==========================================

Benchmark Collections:
- SAT Competition (SATCOMP): Used in 15+ papers
- SATLIB: Used in 5 papers
- G4SATBench: Used in 3 papers (2309.16941, 2504.01173)
- DIMACS: Used in 3 papers

MaxSAT Benchmarks:
- MaxSAT Evaluations: Used in 10+ papers
- Max2SAT, Max3SAT variants

Structured Problem Sets:
- SR series (SR40, SR50, SR60, SR70, SR80, SR90, SR100, SR150, SR200, SR400): Extensively used in 2504.01173
- RB series (RB50, RB100): Random benchmark instances
- UUF series (uuf250-1065): Uniform unsatisfiable instances

Circuit/Hardware:
- LEC (Logic Equivalence Checking): Used in nearly all papers
- Circuit-SAT: Used in 5 papers
- ForgeEDA: Used in 2508.04235
- LECSAT/LECUNSAT: Used in 2508.04235

Graph Problems:
- GSET: Graph benchmark (2208.10227, 2312.11547)
- k-Clique, k-Domset, k-Vercov: Graph-based problems (2309.16941)
- Zamkeller/Zankeller numbers: Number partitioning (2506.11057)

Other:
- BIRD: Database benchmarks (2205.04423, 2510.15583)
- SMTLIB/SMT-LIB: SMT solver benchmarks (2510.15583)
- Anniversary: SAT competition anniversary track (2403.03517, 2405.11024)
- SATzilla: Portfolio solver benchmarks (2211.14405, 2405.11024)
